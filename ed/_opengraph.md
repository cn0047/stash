Open Graph Protocol
-

[doc](http://ogp.me/)

Basic Metadata:
* og:title
* og:type
* og:image
* og:url

Optional Metadata:
* og:description
* og:locale (en_US)

Arrays:

````html
<meta property="og:image" content="http://example.com/rock.jpg" />
<meta property="og:image" content="http://example.com/rock2.jpg" />
````
